Template Usage Example

This demo shows how to properly integrate pydantic-forms with Jinja2 templates:

# In your Flask route:
form_html = render_form_html(MyForm, framework="bootstrap")
return render_template('my_form.html', form_html=form_html)

# In your Jinja2 template:
{{ "{{ form_html|safe }}" }}
